Introduction

Xpress PC was incorporated & established in 2020, and to date is one of the largest Laptop sellers in Sri Lanka. We offer a wide range of Laptops and Ultra-Books, Gaming Laptops, Desktop Computers, Printers, Laptop Spares, Accessories and more. We are counted among the top companies which are engaged in supplying Laptops and Accessories. Because of our vast distribution network, it has always been easy for us to deliver retail as well as bulk orders for Laptop Accessories within the promised time frame. We ensure that we have a collaborative partnership with our customers and that we are always fully up-to-date with the latest technology developments and can provide our customers with the best solutions which meet their unique requirements. We provide high quality services at the most compatible prices which will help our customers to make the right choice as per their budget and requirements.

Our vision is to provide the "Best sales and service experience" to our customers with maximum efficiency and reliability. We give our customers the assurance that we will be there when they need us.
